# PRODUCT.md — HW Media Website

register: brand

## Product Purpose

Portfolio site for HW Media (Harry Wallis) — a London video production and photography studio. The site's job: make brand-side decision-makers feel the craft within 10 seconds, show the work moving, and convert serious enquiries. Design IS the product here.

## Users

- Marketing leads / founders at heritage-leaning brands (automotive, aviation, equestrian, finance, events) evaluating a filmmaker.
- Agencies looking for a reliable director/DP crew.
- Viewing context: desktop in an office or laptop in the evening; phone via Instagram link-outs.

## Brand

- Positioning: cinematic brand storytellers. Heritage-driven, quietly confident, rugged-romantic, craft-led.
- Voice: short declarative sentences. One thought per line. Words: craft, story, heritage, pursuit. Never: leverage, synergy, solutions.
- Visual DNA: black canvas, footage supplies the colour; champagne gold accents used sparingly; warm cream editorial sections; Harry's red handwriting as annotation only; production-craft details (timecode, mono labels) as texture.

## Tone

Cinema first, editorial second. Restrained type scale (the footage is the drama, not 170px fonts — client feedback 2026-06-09). Dynamic motion, never bouncy. Grain, focus pulls, theme flips.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages (gradient heroes, identical card grids).
- Squarespace videographer templates (centered everything, stock icons).
- Crypto-dark neon. Tech-bro "AI-powered" framing.
- Type so large it shouts (BBH scale rejected by client after v1).

## Strategic principles

1. Every section must show moving footage or earn its stillness.
2. Structural variety: no two sections share a layout skeleton.
3. Mono micro-labels vs display type, almost nothing mid-scale.
4. Editorial grid discipline (enerblock reference): rules, columns, restraint.
5. The reel is the hero; UI gets out of its way.
